# Summit Research Plan

Use this skill after intake approval and before autonomous planning or coding.

## Workflow

1. Read `.codex-loop/research/PLAN.md`, `FINDINGS.md`, and `APPROVAL.md`.
2. Break the work into research phases instead of jumping straight to implementation.
3. Name what evidence must be collected and what options should be compared.
4. Record rejected paths and carry-forward risks in `FINDINGS.md`.
5. Lock the recommended direction and staged execution sequence in `APPROVAL.md`.

## Output bar

- The agent has an explicit staged plan instead of a vague goal.
- The real unknowns and evidence needs are visible.
- The approved direction is strong enough to seed tasks without thrashing.
- The later task graph can map cleanly onto the staged execution plan.
